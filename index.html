About Steven

Steven's real estate journey began during his college years with the purchase of his first property, a basement unit condo in need of repair, the most affordable property in the city at the time. This initial investment ignited his passion for real estate. Upon graduating with degrees in Computer Science and Economics, Steven forgoed job offers and bet on himself, going all in on flipping properties. Buying distressed, well located, add value property, and refinancing became the business model.
