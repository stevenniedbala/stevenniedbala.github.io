Strategy

Principles that guide our investments

Prime Locations

Focus on areas with strong population growth, job opportunities, and access to transportation and amenities.

Market Demand

Target markets with growing renter populations and housing shortages.

Add-Value

Invest in properties with operational inefficiencies or physical upgrades opportunities.

Management

Self manage assets to maximize returns.

Exit

2-10 year refinance or sale to return capital.

Repeat

Strategically reinvest proceeds into the next project.
